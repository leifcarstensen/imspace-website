Frequently Asked Questions

What is ImSpace?

ImSpace is an iOS image generation app that turns everyday photos into beautiful new images. Choose from 70+ curated styles, from photoreal and cinematic looks to playful artistic filters.

How does it work?

Take a photo (or pick one from your library), choose a style, adjust detail and creativity settings, and tap create. Your new image is ready in seconds.

What are credits?

Credits are the currency you use to generate images. Each creation costs a specific number of credits, and you always see the cost before you create. You can get credits through subscriptions or one-time credit packs.

Can I try it for free?

Yes! ImSpace is free to download and comes with starter credits so you can try it out. When you're ready for more, choose a subscription or grab a credit pack.

What kind of photos work best?

ImSpace works with almost any clear snapshot: friends, pets, travel scenes, toys, objects, and daily moments. Good lighting and a sharp subject usually produce the best results.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. Your creations stay private, we do not sell your data, and we do not train models on your photos. We are transparent about processing and built privacy into the product from day one.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Of course. All subscriptions are managed through the App Store. You can cancel anytime, and you'll keep access to your plan until the end of your billing period.

Do credits expire?

Credits from one-time credit packs must be used within six months of purchase. Subscription credits roll over to the next month, but expire after two months if unused. New subscription credits are added at the start of each billing cycle.

Which devices are supported?

ImSpace is currently available on iPhone running iOS 17 or later. An iPad and Android version are planned for the future.

Do I need a subscription to use ImSpace?

No. ImSpace does not require a subscription. You can buy credit packs as one-time purchases and pay only for what you create. Subscriptions are available for regular creators who want monthly credits at a lower per-credit rate, but they are entirely optional.

Do I need to write prompts to use ImSpace?

No prompt writing is needed. Simply take a photo or pick one from your library, choose a style from 70+ curated options, and tap create. No technical knowledge or AI experience required.

How is ImSpace different from other AI photo apps?

ImSpace is built around simplicity and fair pricing: no prompts, no mandatory subscription, and no hidden fees. You pay only for what you create. Your photos are never used for AI training, and all processing happens on secure EU servers.

Can ImSpace create professional headshots?

Yes. ImSpace includes a dedicated Headshots & Portraits style category with looks designed for LinkedIn, job applications, and dating profiles. Take a clear selfie in good lighting, pick a headshot style, and your professional portrait is ready in seconds — no studio appointment required.

How many credits does a headshot cost?

A single headshot generation uses the same credits as any other style. With a Credit 100 pack ($4.99), you can create dozens of headshot variations — a fraction of the cost of a traditional photography session.

Can I use ImSpace photos on LinkedIn or for job applications?

Yes. You own 100% of every image you generate in ImSpace. You can use your AI headshots on LinkedIn, in CVs, on company websites, or anywhere else you need a professional photo. Commercial use is included.
