Headshot questions, answered.

How realistic do the AI headshots look?

ImSpace uses state-of-the-art image generation to produce photorealistic portraits. The quality is high enough for LinkedIn profiles and job applications. Best results come from clear, well-lit selfies with a simple background.

Do I need a subscription to get headshots?

No subscription required. You can buy a Credit 100 pack ($4.99) and create dozens of headshot variations as a one-time purchase. Subscriptions are available if you want monthly credits at a lower per-headshot rate, but they're entirely optional.

Can I use my AI headshot on LinkedIn?

Yes. You own 100% of every image you generate. You can use ImSpace headshots on LinkedIn, in your CV, on a company website, or as a profile photo anywhere you need one. Commercial use is included.

What kind of selfie works best?

A clear, well-lit selfie in natural daylight with a simple background produces the best results. Face the camera directly, leave some space around your head, and avoid heavy filters or dark lighting. You don't need to look perfectly polished — the style handles that.

Are my photos used to train AI?

No. Your photos are never used for AI training. They're processed on secure EU servers, deleted after your headshot is generated, and never shared with third parties.

How many headshots can I make?

As many as you like. Each headshot costs a small number of credits. A Credit 100 pack ($4.99) gives you enough to create dozens of variations across different styles. No limit on the number of headshots per session.

Is ImSpace available on Android?

ImSpace is currently available on iPhone (iOS 17 or later). An Android version is planned for the future.

How is ImSpace different from Aragon or HeadshotPro?

Aragon and HeadshotPro require you to upload 10–20 photos, wait hours for model training, and pay $29–$59 upfront. ImSpace is instant — upload one selfie, pick a style, get your headshot in seconds. No training wait. No $30+ fee. Pay only for what you create.
