Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

1. Introduction

VoiceBeam GmbH ("we", "us", "our") operates the ImSpace mobile application and the website imspace.app (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. ImSpace is designed to collect only the minimum amount of data necessary to provide the Service.

2. Data We Collect

When you use ImSpace, we collect and store only the following data:

  • Account information — provided through Sign in with Apple (unique identifier and optional relay email address)
  • Photos you upload — images you choose to transform within the app
  • Generated images — the stylized images created through the Service
  • Purchase history — records of subscriptions and credit pack purchases
  • Credit balance — your current credit count

We do not collect your real name, phone number, location data, device identifiers for advertising, contacts, or browsing history.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide the ImSpace service:

  • To process and transform your uploaded photos into stylized images
  • To manage your credit balance and subscription status
  • To enable you to view and download your generated images
  • To provide customer support when requested

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than providing the core features of the app.

4. Photo Data Handling

Photos you upload are processed to generate your stylized images. Original uploaded photos are not stored permanently and are deleted from our processing servers after your image has been generated. Generated images are stored in your account until you choose to delete them.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure cloud servers. All data is transmitted over encrypted connections using HTTPS/TLS. We follow industry-standard security practices to protect your data from unauthorized access.

6. Third-Party Services

ImSpace uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — for authentication, database storage, and cloud functions.
  • Sign in with Apple — for account authentication.
  • Apple App Store — for subscription and in-app purchase management.
  • Vercel — for hosting the imspace.app website.

We do not share your personal data with any other third parties. We do not sell your data. We do not use advertising networks or tracking services within the app.

7. Data We Do NOT Collect

For clarity, ImSpace does not collect or store:

  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers for advertising (IDFA)
  • Contacts from your address book
  • Browsing history or app usage analytics within the app
  • Financial or payment information (handled by Apple)

8. Website Analytics

The ImSpace website (imspace.app) may use Vercel Web Analytics to measure aggregate page views and visitor counts. This analytics service does not use cookies and does not collect personal data. No analytics or tracking is performed within the ImSpace app itself.

9. Cookies

The ImSpace website does not use cookies for functionality. If website analytics are enabled, they operate without cookies. The ImSpace app does not use cookies.

10. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access — You can view all your stored data within the app.
  • Deletion — You can delete your entire account at any time from within the app. Upon deletion, all your data is permanently removed from our servers.
  • Portability — You can download your generated images at any time.
  • Rectification — You can update your account information at any time within the app.

If you are a resident of the European Union, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

11. Children's Privacy

ImSpace is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

12. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored on servers outside your country of residence. If you are located outside the United States, please be aware that your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using the Service, you consent to this transfer.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
